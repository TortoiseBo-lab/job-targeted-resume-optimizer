# Target JD: UK Graduate Scheme Business Analyst

The graduate analyst will support business operations, research, reporting, and cross-functional projects.

## Responsibilities

- Analyse business performance data.
- Prepare clear reports and presentation materials.
- Support operational improvement projects.
- Work with stakeholders across commercial, operations, and finance teams.

## Requirements

- Strong academic record.
- Analytical and communication skills.
- Familiarity with Excel and presentation tools.
- Interest in business operations and strategy.
