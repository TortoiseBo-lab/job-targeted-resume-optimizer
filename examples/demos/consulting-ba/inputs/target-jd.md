- Conduct market, customer, and competitor research.
- Analyze quantitative and qualitative data.
- Develop client-ready slides and recommendations.
- Work in teams to solve business problems.

- Strong academic record.
- Analytical problem solving and communication skills.
- Experience with Excel, PowerPoint, research, or structured analysis.
- Leadership or teamwork experience.
