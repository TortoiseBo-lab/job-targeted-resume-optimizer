# Target JD: Investment Banking Summer Analyst

The analyst will support deal execution, valuation, industry research, presentation materials, and financial analysis for corporate clients.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare comparable-company analysis and precedent transaction analysis.
- Build and audit valuation models.
- Conduct market, company, and industry research.
- Support pitchbook and client presentation preparation.

## Qualifications

- Strong academic record in finance, economics, accounting, or related field.
- Proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint.
- Clear written communication and attention to detail.
- Interest in investment banking and capital markets.
