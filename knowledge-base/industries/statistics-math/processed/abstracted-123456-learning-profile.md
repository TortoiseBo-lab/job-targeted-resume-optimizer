# Abstracted learning profile: Statistics and Mathematics Resume

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Statistics / Mathematics / Quantitative Research
- Target country: International
- Length: One page unless academic publications or research output justify a CV.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Quantitative Skills
4. Research / Projects
5. Experience
6. Awards
7. Publications

## Writing formula

Action + statistical/math method + dataset/problem + validation metric + interpretation or decision value

## Language style

Rigorous, quantitative, model-aware, and explainable.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
