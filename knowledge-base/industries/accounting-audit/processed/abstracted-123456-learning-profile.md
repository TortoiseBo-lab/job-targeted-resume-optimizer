# Abstracted learning profile: Accounting and Audit Resume

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Accounting / Audit / Assurance
- Target country: International
- Length: One page, conservative and evidence-dense.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Accounting / Finance Experience
4. Projects
5. Certifications
6. Technical Skills
7. Activities

## Writing formula

Action + accounting/audit object + procedure/tool + scope + accuracy, compliance, or reporting outcome

## Language style

Conservative, accurate, process-aware, and compliance-minded.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
