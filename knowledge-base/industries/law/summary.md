# Law and Legal Resume

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Law / Legal / Compliance
- Target country: International
- Length: One page for most legal internships and entry roles; longer CV only for academic/legal scholarship profiles.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Legal Experience
4. Research and Writing
5. Clinic / Moot / Casework
6. Leadership
7. Languages and Skills

## Writing formula

Action + legal material + analytical method + audience/use + quality, decision, or compliance value

## Language style

Formal, analytical, jurisdiction-aware, and restrained.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
