# Abstracted learning profile: Tourism, Hospitality and Service Management Resume

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Tourism / Hospitality / Service Management
- Target country: International
- Length: One page with service, operations, language, and event evidence easy to scan.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Service / Operations Experience
4. Events / Projects
5. Leadership
6. Languages
7. Skills and Certifications

## Writing formula

Action + service/operations context + guest/customer/stakeholder group + scale + satisfaction, efficiency, or reliability outcome

## Language style

Professional, service-oriented, cross-cultural, and operationally reliable.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
