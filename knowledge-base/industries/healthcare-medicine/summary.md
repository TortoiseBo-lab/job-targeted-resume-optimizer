# Medicine, Healthcare and Life Science CV

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Medicine / Healthcare / Life Science
- Target country: International
- Length: One page for internships/jobs; longer academic CV for publications, clinical training, or research output.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Clinical / Research Experience
4. Lab and Technical Skills
5. Projects
6. Publications / Presentations
7. Certifications
8. Volunteer

## Writing formula

Action + clinical/lab/public-health context + method/protocol + population/sample/scope + quality or research output

## Language style

Careful, scientific, ethics-aware, and protocol-specific.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
