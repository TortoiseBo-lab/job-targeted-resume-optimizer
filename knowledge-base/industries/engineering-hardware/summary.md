# Electrical, Mechanical and Hardware Engineering CV

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Electrical / Mechanical / Hardware Engineering
- Target country: International
- Length: One page for internship/job resumes; longer CV only for research-heavy applications.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Technical Skills
4. Engineering Projects
5. Internship / Experience
6. Research / Lab Experience
7. Awards

## Writing formula

Action + engineered object/system + method/tool + constraint/test condition + validated output or performance result

## Language style

Precise, technical, validation-oriented, and restrained.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
