# Abstracted learning profile: Economics and Business Management Resume

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Economics / Business Management
- Target country: International
- Length: One page for early-career roles; keep academic projects concise and decision-focused.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Business / Analytical Experience
4. Projects
5. Leadership
6. Skills
7. Activities

## Writing formula

Action + business/economic question + method/data + deliverable/recommendation + implication or stakeholder value

## Language style

Analytical, business-ready, structured, and plain-spoken.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
