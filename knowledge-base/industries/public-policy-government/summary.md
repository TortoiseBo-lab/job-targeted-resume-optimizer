# Public Policy and Government CV

Abstracted from the newly added docs/sample-resume-knowledge CV sample folder. This node stores role, layout, and language rules only; it does not copy source resume text.

## Best for

- Target role: Policy / Government / Public Sector
- Target country: International
- Length: One page for roles; academic/policy CV may extend when research, publications, or public service justify it.

## Section order

1. Contact
2. Education
3. Policy / Government Experience
4. Research Projects
5. Leadership and Service
6. Publications / Presentations
7. Skills and Languages

## Writing formula

Action + policy/government question + research method/source + output + stakeholder or decision value

## Language style

Analytical, civic, evidence-based, and stakeholder-aware.

## What to preserve

- Domain-specific nouns and evidence types.
- Clear fact boundaries when metrics or authority are missing.
- Conventional ATS-readable structure for export to PDF, Word, and copy.

## Avoid

- responsible for
- helped with
- worked on
- participated in
- various
- many
- good
- excellent
- hardworking
- detail-oriented
